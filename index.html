About Us 😍

Ben and Emily met in June of 2018, right as Ben was transitioning from medical school to residency and Emily was transitioning from classroom teaching to nonprofit work. We bonded over our shared love of books and the outdoors, and immediately began spending too much time together. During our first two months together we would adventure to City Island, Montauk, Coney Island, and every Ample Hills Creamery in the tri-state area. Sharing our studio apartment during the pandemic solidified our love, and we are excited to spend the rest of our lives together laughing together, learning and teaching one another, and supporting each other.
